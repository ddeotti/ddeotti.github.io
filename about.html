About Me

Dr. Eleanor Harper is a distinguished professor in the field of electrical engineering, specializing in renewable energy systems and smart grid technologies. With over 15 years of experience in academia and industry, Dr. Harper has made significant contributions to the advancement of sustainable energy solutions.

Research Interests

Dr. Harper's research interests include the development of advanced control algorithms for smart grids, optimization of renewable energy resources, and the integration of distributed generation systems. Her work focuses on enhancing the efficiency, reliability, and sustainability of modern power grids.
